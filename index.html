Organizations

Serving and leading organizations is energizing. Being a member of these organizations has truly changed my life and created many opportunities for personal growth.

Akshaya Patra UTD

The Akshaya Patra Foundation is a non-profit in India that operates a school lunch program. As vice president of my campus chapter, I currently lead year-round fundraising campaigns to counter classroom hunger and education deprivation in India. My time as an event coordinator and a volunteer chair trained me for managing effective fundraisers.

The Society of Women Engineers UTD

During my freshman year, I had the opportunity to attend the SWE National conference with a scholarship. Since then, I have happily given back to this organization as a conference comittee member and later as vice president - leading an empowering series of workshops and panels.

Phi Beta Lambda

I cultivated my strongest friendships through this organization and its conferences. My passion for marketing competitions has grown immensly as I placed at PBL's national conferences for the past two years. WEHack UTD

WEHack 2020 is my campus' first all women, non-binary hackathon. As the head of the first web development committee, I had the pleasure of leading an Agile, new student developer team to build WEHackUTD.com. I hope to encourage more underrepresented people to harness their skills to create projects of their own.
